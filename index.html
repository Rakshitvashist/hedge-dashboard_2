Strategy Definition

Core Institutional Logic & Defensive Multi-Layer Architecture
📈

1. Base SIM (Portfolio Optimization)

Concept: The foundational quantitative selection engine. We calculate individual stock Betas and Residual Variance (σ²) using the Single Index Model (SIM).

Logic: Stocks are ranked by their Excess Return to Beta (ERB). A mathematical cut-off (Ci) is derived; only stocks providing "alpha" above this threshold are selected.

Risk Control: Individual weights are strictly capped at 10% to ensure diversification.
⚡

2. ST Filter (Momentum Defense)

Concept: A tactical momentum overlay applied to the Base portfolio to filter out weakening assets.

Logic: We execute a monthly audit of the Supertrend (10, 3) indicator for every holding.

Defense: Only "Bullish" state assets are retained. Bearish flips trigger immediate removal to avoid capital erosion.
🛡️

3. EMA Filter (Trend Defense)

Concept: A structural trend filter ensuring the portfolio stays on the right side of the long-term cycle.

Logic: Analysis of the 40-period Exponential Moving Average (EMA) position.

Defense: Retains only stocks trading above EMA-40, shielding the portfolio from structural downtrends.
🔒

4. COMBO Filter (High-Conviction)

Concept: Our premier high-conviction selection layer using a "Double-Lock" validation.

Logic: A stock must clear BOTH the Supertrend (Momentum) and EMA-40 (Trend) filters simultaneously.

Result: Drastically reduces volatility and peak-to-trough drawdowns by focusing on dual-confirmed winners.
💎

5. ULTRA Layer (Alpha Concentration)

Concept: A hyper-concentrated version of the COMBO selection for maximum alpha capture.

Logic: Focuses exclusively on elite performers that have cleared all institutional hurdles with the highest scoring metrics.

Strategic Role: Acts as the baseline for advanced tail-risk hedging strategies.
📉

6. COMBO + Hedge (Active Beta Neutralization)

Concept: Dynamic market risk neutralization using NIFTY Futures to preserve alpha during shocks.

Logic: Constant monitoring of Multi-Timeframe (MTF) alignment (15m, 30m, 1h, 1d).

Hedge Trigger: When all 4 timeframes turn Bearish, we short Nifty Futures proportional to the portfolio Beta.
🆘

7. ULTRA Defense (Tail-Risk Multiplier)

Concept: Aggressive "Black Swan" protection designed to neutralize systemic liquidations.

Logic: Real-time monitoring of Market Breadth (ratio of COMBO stocks vs. Base selection).

Super-Hedge: If breadth drops below 50%, we apply a 2.0x Beta multiplier to the short position for maximum capital protection.
